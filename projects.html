Puzzled Time

Puzzled Time is a project that combines elements of a platform, puzzle and adventure game in a steampunk style. This combination will force the player to strain his mind and take him on an amazing journey beyond time and space. Playing the role of a young time traveler Iris, we will follow the scraps of her life, we will unravel the secrets of the past, future and present.
